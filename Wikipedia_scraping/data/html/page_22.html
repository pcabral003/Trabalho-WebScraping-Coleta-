David Ayala (born 26 July 2002) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami . [ 2 ]

Personal

[ edit ]

Ayala's brother, Andrés , is also a professional footballer. [ 3 ] [ 4 ]
